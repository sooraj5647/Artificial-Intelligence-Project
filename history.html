HISTORY OF AI


1943 :McCullough and Pitts’ invented “artificial neurons”.

1950 : Inventions of Alan Turing’s “Computing machinery and Intelligence”.

1951 : AI was using in Games.

1956 : Dartmouth conference; And the birth of AI.

1965 : Robinson’s complete algorithm for logical reasoning.

1969-79 :Early development of knowledge based systems took place.

1980-88 : Expert system industry booms.

1988 - 93 : Expert system industry busts “AI winter”.

1993 -Present : AI is now using rapidly in different technologies; And is achieving it’s goal.

* The duration between years 1974 to 1980 and between 1987 to 1993 was the first and second AI winter duration respectively. AI winter refers to the time period where computer scientist dealt with a severe shortage of funding from government for AI researches. During AI winters, an interest of publicity on artificial intelligence was decreased.

AI is an old concept but it has become more popular today, Thanks to

1. Increased data volumes
2. Advanced algorithms
3. Improvements in computing power
4. Storage (GPUs)
