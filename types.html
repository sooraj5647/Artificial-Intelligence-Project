TYPES OF AI

AI technologies are categorized by their capacity to mimic human characteristics, the technology they use to do this and their real – world applications.

AI is generally divided into three types :

1. Artificial narrow intelligence (ANI)
2. Artificial general intelligence (AGI)
3. Artificial superintelligence (ASI)

Artificial Narrow Intelligence (ANI) / Weak AI / Narrow AI

Narrow AI is goal-oriented, designed to perform singular tasks or it is s focused on one narrow task. - i.e. facial recognition, speech recognition/voice assistants, driving a car, or searching the internet - and is very intelligent at completing the specific task it is programmed to do.It is widely used for NLP.

Examples of narrow AI:
Google Search
Siri by Apple, Alexa by Amazon, Cortana by Microsoft and other virtual assistants
IBM's Watson
Image / facial recognition software
Email spam filters
Self-driving cars
Google Translate

Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) / Strong AI / Deep AI

AGI is the concept of a machine with general intelligence that mimics human intelligence and/or behaviors, with the ability to learn and apply its intelligence to solve any problem. AGI can think, understand, and act in a way that is indistinguishable from that of a human in any given situation.
It is defined as a machine with the ability to apply intelligence to any problem, rather than just one specific problem.
Strong AI does not exist. Experts predict it may be developed by 2030 or 2045.
